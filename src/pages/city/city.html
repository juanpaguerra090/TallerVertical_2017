Guadalajara Zoo

Route Tequila Express

Enjoy the feeling of losing yourself among the blue agave fields. Discover the origin of the tequila, explore its factories, admire its landscape agavero declared a natural pratrimonio of humanity by UNESCO.


Trip to Chapala lake

The largest natural lake in Mexico, enjoy the nature of the lagoon during our excursion along the river route of Chapala, excellent weather, and the typical village of Ajijic with its community of artists and writers.


Meet The Historical Center

Recently chosen by our users as one of the five most spectacular cathedrals in Mexico, Guadalajara is one of the most emblematic buildings of the Jalisco capital. Its construction began in 1561, but it was not until 1874, when it was added its famous towers lined with tile (after a terrible earthquake down the originals in 1818).
